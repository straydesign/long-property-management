Pricing & Policies

Transparent fees, clear maintenance billing, and defined rules. No hidden costs, no surprises.

Management fee

Monthly management

Competitive % of collected rent

If rent isn't collected, we don't charge a management fee. Final pricing depends on property type, unit count, and scope.

  • Owner reporting and documentation
  • Vendor coordination and scheduling
  • Turn support and rent-ready checklists
  • Regular property check-ins
  • Communication and issue triage

Leasing / tenant placement

Placement fee per new tenant

Covers the work of finding and placing a qualified tenant.

  • Property marketing and listing
  • Tenant screening and background checks
  • Lease preparation
  • Move-in documentation and inspection

What the management fee does NOT include

The following are billed separately so you always see the actual cost:

  • Repairs & maintenance
  • Snow removal (seasonal service)
  • Lawn care (seasonal service)
  • Materials and parts

Maintenance billing

Repairs are billed separately from the management fee. You pay the actual repair cost (labor + materials).

Under your threshold

We may proceed without prior approval to keep the property safe and functional. You receive photo documentation and an itemized invoice after the work is complete.

Over your threshold

We request your approval before work begins, except in emergencies. You'll receive a scope description and estimated cost before we proceed.

Emergency exceptions

Emergency repairs are authorized as needed to prevent property damage or safety risk. Documented and reported immediately after response.

Documentation standard

Every repair includes written notes and photo proof when applicable. Invoices are itemized with labor and materials separated.

Seasonal services scope

Snow removal and lawn care are scoped per property. Here's how they work.

Snow removal

Triggered by accumulation thresholds agreed in advance. Billed per service or on a seasonal plan.

  • Driveway and walkway clearing
  • Snowblowing for larger areas
  • Ice management as needed
  • Response within defined window after snowfall

Lawn care

Regular seasonal schedule. Scope defined at onboarding to prevent unexpected charges.

  • Scheduled mowing
  • Basic exterior upkeep
  • Consistent schedule you can rely on
  • Scope adjustments by request only

Pricing FAQ

Do I pay if the unit is vacant?

No. The management fee is based on collected rent. If rent isn't collected, we don't charge.

Can I set my own maintenance approval threshold?

Yes. During onboarding, we agree on a dollar threshold. Repairs below that amount can proceed without prior approval. Above that, we contact you first (except emergencies).

Are there any hidden fees?

No. You pay the management fee, placement fee when applicable, and actual repair/service costs. Everything is documented and itemized.

How is snow removal priced?

Snow removal can be billed per service event or as a seasonal flat-rate plan. We'll recommend the best option based on your property layout and typical Erie snow patterns.
